History

TechCon was established in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since evolved into one of the world's premier tech conferences. Over the past 14 years, it has grown from a local meetup of 200 participants to an international event attracting over 10,000 attendees annually, consistently adapting to showcase emerging technologies from cloud computing to artificial intelligence.

Mission

TechCon is guided by three core principles: fostering innovation through knowledge sharing, building inclusive communities where diverse voices are heard, and promoting sustainable technology practices. Our goals include bridging the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation, creating networking opportunities that spark collaborations, and empowering attendees with skills that drive meaningful progress in their organizations and communities
